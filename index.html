About GeoGensan

Your city,
on the move.

GeoGensan is a navigation and fare calculation app built specifically for General Santos City. No more guessing if your trike driver charged the right fare — just open the app and know before you board.

Powered by real-time GPS, official city ordinance rates, and OSRM routing, GeoGensan gives every commuter in GenSan the tools they deserve.
