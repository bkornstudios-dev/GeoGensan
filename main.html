About

What is this?

GeoGensan helps you plan trips and calculate transportation costs in General Santos City with real-time route mapping based on City Ordinance No. 08, Series of 2023.

Features

Trike Fares

Distance-based pricing with discounts & multi-passenger support

2 Route Options

Shortest route + 1 alternative shown on the map

Live Location

GPS follows you in real-time as you move

ETA Display

Estimated travel time for trike and bus

Bus Routes

View popular jeepney routes with stop details

Report System

Report overcharging or reckless driving

Special Discounts (20% off)

  • PWD: Persons with Disability (with valid ID)
  • Senior Citizens: 60 years old and above (with valid ID)
  • Students: With valid school ID
